NFPA 20

Standard for the Installation of Stationary Pumps for Fire Protection

Issuing body
NFPA
Latest edition
2025
Region
USA, Global
Equipment items
1

Scope

Fire water pump installation, controllers, drivers, testing.

Key requirements (8)

  • Stationary fire-protection pumps — electric, diesel, steam-driven.
  • Pump types: horizontal split-case, end-suction, vertical-inline, vertical-turbine, multistage.
  • Performance: minimum 100% rated flow at 65% rated head and 150% rated flow at 65% head (rough rule).
  • Listed/labeled pumps (UL, FM) required.
  • Diesel driver: dedicated fuel for ≥ 8 hours; 2 batteries; weekly run test.
  • Jockey pump (pressure maintenance): not for fire flow — sized to maintain system pressure.
  • Suction conditions: positive head preferred; flooded suction with ≥ 150% rated capacity available.
  • Annual full-flow testing required.

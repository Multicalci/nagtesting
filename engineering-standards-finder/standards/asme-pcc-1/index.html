Guidelines for Pressure Boundary Bolted Flange Joint Assembly

Scope

Practices for assembling bolted flange joints; bolt-tightening sequences and torque calculations.

Key requirements (7)

  • Bolted flange joint assembly guidelines (post-construction).
  • Pre-tension target: typically 50–60% of bolt yield (varies with gasket type).
  • Bolt-up sequence: cross-pattern in 4–5 passes (25%, 50%, 100% of target torque, then circular passes).
  • Lubrication critical: K-factor of nut factor varies 5–10× with lube quality.
  • Joint qualification: training + practical testing per Appendix A.
  • Re-torque after 24 hours due to gasket creep / relaxation.
  • Hydraulic tensioning preferred for studs > 1" (more even loading).

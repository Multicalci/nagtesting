IEC 61508

Functional safety of electrical/electronic/programmable electronic safety-related systems

Issuing body
IEC
Latest edition
2010
Region
Global
Equipment items
1

Scope

Generic functional safety standard; basis of SIL determination.

Key requirements (6)

  • Functional safety of electrical/electronic/programmable electronic safety-related systems (parent of 61511).
  • Safe Failure Fraction (SFF) = (λS + λDD) / (λS + λDD + λDU).
  • PFH (high demand) ranges: SIL 1 (10⁻⁶–10⁻⁵), SIL 2 (10⁻⁷–10⁻⁶), SIL 3 (10⁻⁸–10⁻⁷), SIL 4 (10⁻⁹–10⁻⁸).
  • Type A vs Type B components — different SFF requirements.
  • Systematic capability (SC1–SC4) certified by independent assessor.
  • Common-cause beta factor typical 5–10 % unless explicit measures taken.

ASME B16.47

Large Diameter Steel Flanges: NPS 26 through NPS 60

Issuing body
ASME
Latest edition
2020 Edition
Region
USA, Global
Equipment items
1

Scope

Series A (formerly MSS SP-44) and Series B (formerly API 605) large-diameter flanges.

Key requirements (6)

  • Large-diameter steel flanges — NPS 26 through 60.
  • Series A (MSS SP-44 lineage, heavier) or Series B (API 605, lighter).
  • Pressure classes 150, 300, 400, 600, 900.
  • Used for pipelines, large vessels, atmospheric tanks.
  • Hydrotest: per pressure class rating chart.
  • Bolting per ASTM A193 / A194; Series A typically larger studs than Series B.

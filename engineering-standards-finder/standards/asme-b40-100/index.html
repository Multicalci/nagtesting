ASME B40.100

Pressure Gauges and Gauge Attachments

Issuing body
ASME
Latest edition
2013 (R2018)
Region
USA, Global
Equipment items
1

Scope

Pressure gauges, transmitters, gauge attachments — accuracy classes, dial sizes, connections.

Key requirements (7)

  • Pressure gauges — Bourdon and diaphragm types.
  • Accuracy grades: 4A (±0.10%), 3A (±0.25%), 2A (±0.5%), 1A (±1%), A (±2/1/2%), B (±3/2/3%), 4 (±5%).
  • Standard dial sizes: 1.5, 2, 2.5, 3, 4, 4.5, 6, 8.5, 12, 16 inches.
  • Range selection: normal operating pressure should be in middle 50% of scale.
  • Pulsation: snubber required for fluctuating service.
  • Liquid-filled (glycerin) gauges damp vibration; preferred for reciprocating pump discharge.
  • Diaphragm seals (chemical seals) protect gauge from corrosive/viscous media.

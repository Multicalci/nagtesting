ASTM A182 / A182M

Forged or Rolled Alloy and Stainless Steel Pipe Flanges, Forged Fittings, and Valves and Parts for High-Temperature Service

Issuing body
ASTM
Latest edition
A182/A182M-23
Region
USA, Global
Equipment items
0

Scope

F11/F22/F91 (alloy) and F304/F316 (stainless) forgings.

Key requirements (6)

  • Forged or rolled alloy/stainless steel pipe flanges, forged fittings, valves and parts for high-temperature service.
  • Grades F1, F11, F22, F91 (alloy); F304, F316, F321, F347 (stainless).
  • Allowable temperatures matched to corresponding A335 pipe grades.
  • Heat treatment: matched to grade (normalized, Q&T, solution annealed).
  • Used for high-temperature flanges and fittings in power, refinery, petrochemical.
  • NDE per ASTM A609 or per project spec for critical service.

Alarm system lifecycle, philosophy, rationalization, KPIs.
Key requirements (6)
  • Alarm management lifecycle.
  • Alarm rationalization: every alarm has a defined cause, consequence, response, time to respond.
  • Alarm rate target: ≤ 1 alarm/10 min steady state (≤ 6/hr) per operator.
  • During upset: ≤ 10 alarms in first 10 minutes.
  • Alarm priority distribution: ~80% low, ~15% medium, ~5% high (rough industry guideline).
  • Suppress alarms in startup/shutdown/maintenance (state-based).

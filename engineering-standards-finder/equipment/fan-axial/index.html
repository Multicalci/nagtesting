Applicable Standards (3)

API — 2 standards

API 661 Petroleum, Petrochemical, and Natural Gas Industries — Air-Cooled Heat Exchangers
Edition: 8th Edition (2022) Region: USA, Global
Air-cooled heat exchangers for refining/petchem/gas. Identical to ISO 13706.
Key requirements (9)
  • Air-cooled heat exchangers (ACHE / fin-fan) for petroleum, petrochemical, gas.
  • Forced draft (fan below) or induced draft (fan above) — induced for better air distribution but higher fan temperature.
  • Tube types: embedded (G-fin) — high temp; extruded bimetal — medium; L-foot/wrapped — low temp / low-cost.
  • Standard tube: 1" OD with 11 fpi (fins per inch), 1/2" fin height typical.
  • Air-side velocity: typically 3–4 m/s face velocity.
  • Fan blade tip speed limit: typically ≤ 60 m/s.
  • Plenum: transition + nozzle; minimum dimensional ratios per Section 5.6.
  • Vibration: fan natural frequency well separated from rotational frequency.
  • Louvers / variable-pitch fans for temperature control in cold climates.

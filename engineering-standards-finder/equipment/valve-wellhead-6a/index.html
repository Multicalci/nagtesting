Key requirements (8)
  • Wellhead, christmas tree and choke valve equipment for upstream oil & gas.
  • Pressure ratings: 2,000 / 3,000 / 5,000 / 10,000 / 15,000 / 20,000 psi.
  • Material classes: AA, BB, CC, DD, EE, FF, HH (increasing severity for sour/cryogenic).
  • Temperature classes: K, L, N, P, R, S, T, U, V, X, Y (range −75 to 350 °F).
  • PSL (Product Specification Level) 1 to 4 — increasing QC stringency.
  • Hydrotest: 1.5 × rated working pressure × 3 minutes minimum.
  • Sour service: NACE MR0175 / ISO 15156 compliance.
  • Fire-safe per API 6FA where required.

Orifice Metering of Natural Gas and Other Related Hydrocarbon Fluids

Issuing body
AGA
Latest edition

USA, Global
Equipment items

Scope

Custody transfer orifice metering of natural gas (US/global default).

Key requirements (8)

  • Custody transfer orifice metering of natural gas (also as API MPMS Ch. 14.3).
  • Concentric square-edged orifice plate per ANSI 2530.
  • Flange or pipe (D, D/2) tap configurations.
  • Mass/volume flow per equation Q = N·Cd·E·Y·d²·√(ρ·ΔP).
  • Beta ratio range: 0.1 ≤ β ≤ 0.75.
  • Plate inspection: bore diameter ±0.05%, edge sharpness < 0.0004·d radius.
  • AGA-8 used for gas density (compressibility).
  • Uncertainty: 0.4–1.0 % when calibrated properly.

Steel Castings, Carbon, Suitable for Fusion Welding, for High-Temperature Service

Scope

Cast carbon steel valve bodies and fittings (WCA, WCB, WCC).

Key requirements (6)

  • Steel castings, carbon, suitable for fusion welding, for high-temperature service.
  • Grades WCA, WCB, WCC: WCB most common (yield 36 ksi, UTS 70 ksi).
  • Service temperature: up to ~425 °C typical.
  • Used for valve bodies, pump casings, fittings.
  • Heat treatment: full annealing, normalizing, or normalize-temper.
  • Soundness inspection (RT, MT, PT) per supplementary requirements.

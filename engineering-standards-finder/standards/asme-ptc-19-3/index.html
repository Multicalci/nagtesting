ASME PTC 19.3 TW

Thermowells — Performance Test Codes

Scope

Strength and stability calculation of thermowells (vortex shedding, frequency, stress).

Key requirements (8)

  • Thermowells — design and stress analysis (also as ASME PTC 19.3 TW).
  • Velocity-induced vibration: avoid resonance (natural frequency ≥ 80% of vortex shedding frequency × scale factor).
  • Strouhal number: 0.22 typical for cylinder in cross flow.
  • Stress check: bending + drag + axial — combined < material allowable.
  • Wake frequency formula (Strouhal): fw = St·V/D where D = thermowell tip diameter.
  • Frequency ratio limit: scaling factor 0.4 (lock-in zone) to 0.8 (resonance).
  • Mounting: threaded, flanged, socket-weld, welded.
  • Insertion length (U): immersion depth into pipe; balance of response time vs vibration.

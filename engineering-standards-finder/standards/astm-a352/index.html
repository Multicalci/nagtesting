ASTM A352 / A352M

Steel Castings, Ferritic and Martensitic, for Pressure-Containing Parts, Suitable for Low-Temperature Service

Issuing body
ASTM
Latest edition
A352/A352M-22
Region
USA, Global
Equipment items
0

Scope

Low-temp cast steel (LCB, LC1, LC2, LC3, LC9).

Key requirements (6)

  • Steel castings, ferritic and martensitic, for pressure-containing parts at low service temperature.
  • Grades LCB, LCC, LC1, LC2, LC3, LC4, LC9: by impact test temperature.
  • LCC: −46 °C MDMT (most common).
  • LC9 (9Ni): down to −195 °C — cryogenic service.
  • Charpy impact at MDMT: per grade table.
  • Used for cast valve bodies, pump casings in cryogenic service.

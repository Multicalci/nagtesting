ASTM — 4 standards

ASTM C547 Mineral Fiber Pipe Insulation
Edition: C547-19 Region: USA, Global
Pre-formed mineral fiber pipe insulation up to about 650 °C surface temperature.
Key requirements (6)
  • Mineral fiber pipe insulation (rock wool, glass wool).
  • Service temperature classes: Class 1 (≤ 230 °C), Class 2 (≤ 450 °C), Class 3 (≤ 540 °C), Class 4 (≤ 650 °C).
  • Thermal conductivity: ~0.038 W/m·K at 24 °C.
  • Density: 70–160 kg/m³ depending on grade.
  • Combined with metallic jacketing for outdoor service.
  • Not water-resistant — vapor barrier required for cold service.
ASTM C612 Mineral Fiber Block and Board Thermal Insulation
Edition: C612-23 Region: USA, Global
Mineral wool / rockwool block and board for vessels, ducts, equipment.
ASTM C585 Inner and Outer Diameters of Thermal Insulation for Nominal Sizes of Pipe and Tubing
Edition: C585-10 (R2021) Region: USA, Global
Standardized pipe-insulation sizing tables.
ASTM C795 Specification for Thermal Insulation for Use in Contact with Austenitic Stainless Steel
Edition: C795-08 (R2018) Region: USA, Global
Insulation chloride / fluoride leachable limits to prevent ESCC of stainless steel.

CINI Manual Insulation for Industries (CINI Manual)
Edition: Living manual Region: Europe, Global
Dutch industrial insulation manual — hot, cold, cryogenic, fire-protection, personnel-protection. Widely adopted across Europe and global EPC projects.
Key requirements (6)

ISO 5167 (series)

Measurement of fluid flow by means of pressure differential devices inserted in circular cross-section conduits running full

Issuing body
ISO
Latest edition
Parts 1–6, 2003–2022
Region
Global
Equipment items
1

Scope

Orifice plates, nozzles, Venturi tubes, cone meters, wedge meters — flow measurement using DP.

Key requirements (8)

  • Differential pressure flow measurement: orifice, nozzle, Venturi.
  • Orifice: square-edged; β ratio (d/D): 0.10–0.75 typical; thickness ≤ 0.05·D.
  • Pressure tap configurations: corner, D and D/2, flange.
  • Discharge coefficient C: Reader-Harris/Gallagher equation for orifice.
  • Expansibility ε ≤ 1.0 for compressible flow correction.
  • Uncertainty: ~0.5–1.0% of rate for properly installed orifice.
  • Upstream straight length: 10–80×D depending on β and disturbance type.
  • Reynolds number range: typically Re_D > 5000 for orifice validity.

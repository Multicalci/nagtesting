ASTM A370

Standard Test Methods and Definitions for Mechanical Testing of Steel Products

Latest edition

Equipment items

Scope

Tensile, hardness, impact (Charpy V-notch), and bend test methods for steels.

Key requirements (7)

  • Standard test methods and definitions for mechanical testing of steel products.
  • Tension test: yield, UTS, elongation, reduction of area.
  • Charpy V-notch impact: 5/10/27/40 J typical acceptance per service.
  • Hardness: Brinell, Rockwell B/C, Vickers conversions.
  • Bend test: typically 180° around mandrel of specified diameter.
  • Test specimen geometries for plate, pipe, bar, structural shapes.
  • Referenced by virtually every ASTM steel material spec.

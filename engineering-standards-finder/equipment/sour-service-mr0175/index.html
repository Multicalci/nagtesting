Applicable Standards (2)

ISO — 1 standard

ISO 15156 (series) / NACE MR0175 Petroleum and natural gas industries — Materials for use in H2S-containing environments in oil and gas production
Edition: Parts 1–3, 2020 Region: Global
Sour service material requirements for upstream — SSC, HIC, SOHIC, SZC resistance.
Key requirements (8)
  • Materials for sour service (H2S) in oil & gas production — also as NACE MR0175.
  • Sour service threshold: H2S partial pressure ≥ 0.05 psia (0.34 kPa).
  • Carbon steel hardness limit: 22 HRC (250 HV) max — prevents SSC.
  • PWHT mandatory for CS in sour service; hardness verified.
  • Materials qualification: laboratory test per NACE TM0177 (Method A, B, C, D) at relevant H2S/Cl/pH.
  • Region 1: SSC (sulfide stress cracking), Region 2: SOHIC, Region 3: HIC.
  • 13 % Cr martensitic (S13Cr): qualified for sour service with H2S limits.
  • Duplex stainless (UNS S31803/S32750): qualified by environmental severity.

NACE/AMPP — 1 standard

NACE MR0103 / ISO 17945 Petroleum, petrochemical, and natural gas industries — Metallic materials resistant to sulfide stress cracking in corrosive petroleum refining environments
Edition: 2016 Region: USA, Global
Refining sour service — material requirements for SSC resistance in process units.
Key requirements (7)
  • Materials for sour refinery process service (downstream complement of ISO 15156).
  • Wet H2S service definition: water + H2S (any concentration in liquid water).
  • Carbon steel hardness limit: 200 HB (HV 215) max in HAZ and weld.
  • PWHT required for all welds in sour service.
  • Charpy impact: 27 J avg / 21 J single at MDMT.
  • Materials chemistry: Pcm ≤ 0.25, CE ≤ 0.43 typical for HIC resistance.
  • HIC-resistant plate testing per NACE TM0284 (CLR ≤ 15%, CTR ≤ 5%, CSR ≤ 2%).

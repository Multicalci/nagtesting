Scope

Dutch industrial insulation manual — hot, cold, cryogenic, fire-protection, personnel-protection. Widely adopted across Europe and global EPC projects.

Key requirements (6)

  • Dutch / European specification for industrial insulation manual.
  • Sheet 2.1.01 onward: design data for hot insulation.
  • Sheet 3.1.01 onward: cold insulation including cryogenic.
  • Vapor barrier integrity test methods for cold service.
  • Materials: cellular glass, PIR, mineral wool, aerogel guidance.
  • Joint sealing details for cold insulation continuity.

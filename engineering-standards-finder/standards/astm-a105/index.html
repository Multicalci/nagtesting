ASTM A105 / A105M

Carbon Steel Forgings for Piping Applications

Issuing body
ASTM
Latest edition
A105/A105M-21
Region
USA, Global
Equipment items
0

Scope

Forged carbon steel components — flanges, fittings, valves, parts — for ambient and high-temperature piping.

Key requirements (7)

  • Carbon steel forgings for piping applications.
  • Yield strength: 36 ksi minimum; UTS: 70 ksi minimum.
  • Service temperature: −29 °C to 425 °C (−20 °F to 800 °F) typical.
  • Used for forged flanges, fittings, valves up to NPS 4 typically.
  • Charpy impact: not required by default; can be supplementary.
  • Heat treatment: as-forged, normalized, or normalized + tempered.
  • Companion forged grade: A350 LF2 for low-temperature service.
